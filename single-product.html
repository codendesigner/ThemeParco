Theme Parco – Digital Marketplace WooCommerce Theme

Theme Parco is a powerful and modern WordPress theme designed for selling digital products, services, and more. Built on WordPress and the world's most popular eCommerce plugin, WooCommerce, this theme provides you with a complete and curated platform to launch your online store.

With Theme Parco, you can effortlessly set up a store and sell digital goods such as stock photos, themes, plugins, software, music, icons, fonts, and more. It has been developed for both single vendor and multivendor stores. You can also allow anyone to upload their digital items and sell them through your site by easily integrating the powerful Dokan multivendor plugin.

Dokan Multivendor Supported

Whether you're just starting or scaling with Dokan, we’ve got you. The plugin offers an exceptional online shop experience and allows multiple vendors to sell right out of the box. It provides a withdrawal system, vendor dashboards, store pages, coupons, shipping, earnings, and more.
